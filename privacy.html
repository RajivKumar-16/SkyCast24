Privacy Policy

Application : SkyCast24
Developer   : Rajiv Kumar Gond

1. Location Access

SkyCast24 may request permission to access your location to provide accurate local weather data. This data is used only in your browser and never stored or shared.

2. City Search

When you enter a city name, it is sent to OpenStreetMap’s Nominatim API to retrieve coordinates. SkyCast24 does not collect or store this data.

3. Third-Party Services

SkyCast24 uses the following third-party APIs:

  • Open-Meteo (Weather Data)
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim (Geocoding)

These services may log basic request metadata. Refer to their privacy policies for more details.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking scripts. Your browsing activity is not monitored.

5. Personal Information

SkyCast24 does not collect names, emails, or personal identifiers. No registration or login is required.

6. Data Security

No personal data is collected or stored, ensuring your information stays private and secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Updates will be posted on this page.
